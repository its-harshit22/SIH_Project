Platform Features

Farmer Registration

Farmers can register using a unique identification number and link their bank accounts for direct benefit transfers.

Weather & Satellite Data

Real-time weather data and satellite imagery to detect natural hazards and verify claims automatically.

Complaint Filing & Verification

Farmers can upload evidence of crop damage. AI will help verify claims, with physical inspections if necessary.

Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT)

Compensation is directly transferred to the farmer’s bank account after verification, eliminating intermediaries.

Training & Awareness Programs

Workshops and campaigns to educate farmers on using the platform effectively.

Feedback Mechanism

Farmers can provide feedback to help continuously improve the platform.
